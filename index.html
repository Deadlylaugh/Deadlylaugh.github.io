Hello everybody, I'm Justin! I'm a new grad from University of California, Santa Cruz with a major in Computer Science: Game Design. I am interested in Game AI, UI/UX Design, and Game Systems. I have had a passion for Game Design ever since the first game I played, Pokemon Red, when I was nine years old. Since then I have a fascination with the process of making and analyzing games through the lens of a game designer.

I was able to gain a great amount of experience from my internship at Hangar 13 Games and my continued participation of UCSC Game Development club. Being able to experience the work environment of the game industry through Hangar 13 was such a pleasure since it gave me confidence that making games was what I want to do in life. I spent an entire summer designing and crafting abilities and AI test maps, while also honing my coding skills. This not only prepared me for future game design roles, but also helped me figure out certain aspects of game design that really stood out to me.

Since then, I was very fortunate to get hired by Escape Velocity Games right after graduating college. Throughout my time with them, I have gained so many skills and experience to my game development journey, especially growth as a combat designer. Having to ideate and implement a protoype character within two weeks really streched my imagination and pushed the boundaries of my game development capabilites. I am also grateful to be mentored by two amazing senior game designers which allowed me to learn from my many, many mistakes and quickly see improvements in myself through those iterations.

Feel free to ask me anything or reach out to me at jaq1401@gmail.com!
